# Community Code of Conduct

OpenFunnels welcomes contributors and users of every background and experience level.

Be respectful, constructive, and specific. Assume good intent, critique ideas rather than people, respect privacy, and do not harass, threaten, discriminate, or publish another person's private information. Maintainers may edit or remove harmful content and restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository maintainers or GitHub's reporting tools. Good-faith reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.
